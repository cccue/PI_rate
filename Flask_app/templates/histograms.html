Insight 103; meaning behind bars: Probability distributions are the easiest way to understanding rum quality when analyzing review data, with individual brand rating distributions already shipping within RumRatings. However, better understanding of quality can only be achieved when individual distributions are compared to the overall rating distribution of all types, as only the overall rating distribution can address the question: what does the average rum look like?

The tool introduced below allows the user to generate the rating and sentiment analysis distributions for any rum while comparing them to the overall distros for the full site. Sentiment analysis was done with the help of the Indico API.

Enter reference rum index below (Click here for rum index list) to build the rating and sentiment distribution histograms. In both cases reference distributions are compared to the overall site distributions.
